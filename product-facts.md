# Product Facts

> Verified and scoped on 2026-06-14.

## Product Scope

- This is an unofficial entertainment personality quiz.
- It does not claim an official relationship with FIFA, national teams, clubs, or players.
- A result maps the user's answers to a public on-pitch playing trait. It does not judge or infer a player's complete personal personality.

## Asset Policy

- No official tournament logo.
- No national-team or club crest.
- No real player portrait.
- No reproduction of an official trading-card layout.
- The footballer artwork is an original anonymous generated illustration.

## Result Naming

The named footballers are used as recognizable examples of public playing styles. The product copy must avoid implying endorsement or official participation.

## Visual Source

- Selected concept: `public/assets/reference-collector-card.png`
- Original anonymous footballer artwork: `public/assets/player-blank.png`
- The jersey artwork is numberless. Homepage and result numbers are rendered dynamically so the visible shirt number matches the selected result.
